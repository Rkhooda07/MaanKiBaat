### Most Reliable Option: Fresh OpenAI Account

## Step 1: Create New OpenAI Account
1. Go to https://platform.openai.com/signup
2. Use a new email (different from your current account)
3. Complete verification
4. You'll get  FREE credit automatically

## Step 2: Get API Key
1. Go to https://platform.openai.com/api-keys
2. Click 'Create new secret key'
3. Copy the key (starts with sk-...)

## Step 3: Update .env
OPENAI_API_KEY=sk-your_new_key_here

- ✅ Fresh  credit (enough for ~50,000 messages)
